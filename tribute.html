Tribute to Sindhutai Sapkal

"The Mother Of Orphans"

14 November 1948 - 4 January 2022

Biography

Sindhutai Sapkal was born on 14th November 1948 at Pimpri Meghe Village,Wardha District Maharashtra.
Sindhutai Sapkal was an Indian social worker and social activist.
She was known for her work in raising orphaned children in India.
She was awarded the Padma Shri in 2021.
She took care of over 1,500 orphaned children.
She had a grand family of 382 sons-in-law and 49 daughters-in-law.
She used award money to buy land to make a home for orphaned children.
She was died on 4 January 2022
